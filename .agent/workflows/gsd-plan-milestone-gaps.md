## Step 1: Load Audit Results

Read the most recent `MILESTONE-AUDIT.md`. Parse unsatisfied requirements, missing connections, and broken flows.

## Step 2: Prioritize Gaps

Group gaps by priority from `REQUIREMENTS.md` (must, should, nice).

## Step 3: Group Gaps into Phases

Cluster related gaps by affected phase or subsystem. Keep phases focused (2-4 tasks each).

## Step 4: Determine Phase Numbers

New phases continue from the highest existing phase number in the roadmap.

## Step 5: Present Gap Closure Plan

Present proposed phases and which gaps they close. Ask for user approval.

## Step 6: Update Roadmap & directories

Add new phases to `ROADMAP.md` with gap closure context. Create the phase directories.

## Step 7: Commit & Offer Next

Commit changes and suggest `/plan-phase {N}` to start the first gap closure phase.
